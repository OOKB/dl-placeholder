DeLany & Long is a small, focused company, responsive to our customers and committed to quality service. Our combination of exceptional service and product availability makes us unique in the industry. Our Customer Service department is directed and staffed by experienced professionals. All of our fabrics are woven to order for us and we maintain a large inventory at our Greenwich, Connecticut location. Business is conducted on a pro forma basis and most fabrics can be shipped immediately.
